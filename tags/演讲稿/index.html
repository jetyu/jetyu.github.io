ToastMasters:Timer - 1st

2017/04/22 Dalian No.1 TMC Regular Meeting

English Meeting

介绍

Dear my fellow toastmasters and distiguished guests,
I am timer for today’s meeting,my responsebility is keep tracking time for every speaker,next I will introduce timing rules.I will show three corresponding colour cards until end your speech.

  • for Table Topic Speech(1’-2’)
    1’00” - I will show Green Card,it means qualified
    1’30’ - I will show Yellow Card,it means 30” left
    2’00” - I will show Red Card.When you see the red card you must wrap up your speech or review as quickly as possible.
    TimeOut - When you over 2’15”,I will press the bell to remind you time out.

  • for prepared speech(5’-7’)
    5’00” - I will show Green Card,it means qualified
    6’00” - I will show Yellow Card,it means 1’ left
    7’00” - I will show Red Card.When you see the red card you must wrap up your speech as quickly as possible.
    TimeOut - When you over 7’30”,I will press the bell to remind you time out.

  • for other role-takers,if you time out, I will directly show the Red card.

At the end of the meeting, I will present my report for everyone. For now, please enjoy the meeting!

点评

I now announce the time used by each speaker.

For Table Topics (2 mins), two minutes is the maximum time allowed for each speaker. Cecilia is one minute and twenty seconds, Derek is two minutes and fifty seconds, Jacky is One Minute and five seconds.

Feedbacks: Seems like Derek could speak shorter while Jacky should speak at least one minute and thirty seconds

For Prepared Speech (7 mins), Agnes is six minutes and twenty seconds for her seven-minute speech, Josephine is six minutes and ten seconds for her seven-minute speech and Lilan is fourteen minutes for her fifteen minutes advanced speech.

Feedbacks: Everyone did a great job in fulfilling their project requirements, well done!

Don’t forget *Evaluators, it will be the same as above in reporting the time first and give feedbacks if necessary for good use of time or overtime (expect over time for many evaluators).

总结

  • 值得肯定的点：舞台身体动作不错，熟练。
  • 需要改善的点：身体动作有些多，没有全部面向观众

ToastMasters:Project 2 - Organize Your Speech

2017/04/08 Dalian No.1 TMC Regular Mandarin Meeting

正文

要么瘦，要么死

（引出话题，要么瘦要么死）
以前看小S的这句话，只觉极度的残忍，因为那个时候的我是个胖子，我觉得人为什么要为难自己，该吃吃，该喝喝，但是周围的人总会用这样的话语来嘲讽你，尤其上学的时候，我相信这些话一定都伤过很多胖子，最终呢我们听的多了，也就有了免疫力了。

（减肥的初衷）
但是说起近些年减肥的初衷呢，是这张2015年11月21日，我在朋友圈分享的照片，我的朋友们盖楼似的回复了一句话：你怎么又胖了！！刚开始的时候呢，我也就一笑了之，随后我就在想，我胖，我吃你家大米了？但是那天晚上躺倒床上的时候，就想，我是不是该真正的去改变一下自己了。所以在那天开始，我决定了，我这次一定要减肥成功。

（减肥成功之后带来的身体外貌变化）
于是我走进了健身房，请了私教，开始了我的减肥之旅。经过了1年多的锻炼，主要有2个方面的变化：容貌，身材。

首先说容貌吧，容貌的变化也是最大的。这是87kg的时候，65kg的时候，现在我基本在63Kg左右。有一天我在Facebook上发照片，我朋友评论了一下，你整容了啊？其实这种窃窃的喜悦感，只有自己知道。

其次是身体的变化，因为我14年的时候查出来，我的肝功，血脂都不正常，当时大夫怀疑我是喝酒太多，然后我就戒酒了，15年的时候，还是这个样子，又换了大夫，问了一下我的职业，又看了一下我的体重，就说了一句话：你太胖了，减肥去吧，你们干IT的都这样，我遇见好多了。所以减下来之后，在去年检查的时候，指标全部正常，非常健康。这是我体重的变化图。可以看得出，我是从85KG左右开始减的，然后到最近的63KG左右，有一段时间是有平台期的，但是过了之后，减肥效率就很高了。差不多到现在为止减了22KG左右，大家可能没啥概念，我网上找了一张图，这坨10斤，这坨10斤，这坨20斤，这三坨加起来是我减掉的重量。然后你想想一下这些肉在你身上的样子。以前胖的时候啊，照相是这样的。后来照相，画风都是这样的。胖的时候别人一说拍照我就躲，因为我知道我怎么拍都丑，脸都是肿的，衣服只能买大码的，不低头双下巴都会出来，我无法接受那个时候的我。

（瘦下来之后大家带来态度的变化）
减肥成功，带给我的不仅仅是形象的提升，无形中带给了我很多光环。可以用一句话来形容，人瘦了，天亮了。首先是周围人的态度发生了很大的变化，恶意没那么多了，鄙视没那么多了，压力没那么大了，对我自己而言，带来的变化已然内化于心，上楼不喘了，社交不虚了，敢爱敢恨了，结果单身了。随之经历的越来越多，内心也越来越充实，自己也越来越强大。以至于，当我回头看走过的这条路时，更多的是一种欣慰，更多的是一种坦然。尝试过了，你才会发现，胖是一种情怀，减肥只是一种选择。不可回避的说，这种选择会为你带来一些美好的回忆，小小成就大大满足感。我现在看到很多美貌被肥肉遮盖的人，如果他是自信的自爱的，那我觉得他在健康允许的范围内不减肥也可以，但如果他像曾经的我一样郁郁寡欢，自暴自弃，我由衷的希望他减肥成功。因为当你变得更好的时候，你会发现世界也会变得更加美丽。最后我想把这张图送给大家，塑造自己，过程很疼，但你最终能收获一个更好的自己。

表现

总结

  • 值得肯定的点：舞台身体动作不错，熟练。
  • 需要改善的点：身体动作有些多，没有全部面向观众

ToastMasters:Project 1 - The Ice Breaker

2017/03/04 Dalian No.1 TMC Regular English Meeting

正文

  Before today’s speech, I have a question to ask all. two months have been gone in this year, are you ready to start your New Year’s resolutions? It seems that lots of people started new year’s resolutions. OK, different people have different answers.
  For my point of view. I am convinced that New Year’s resolutions are the perfect opportunities for those who have failed to start making changes that they said they would make next week, next month, or perhaps when winter is coming. so we should work out resolutions so that you begin to change ourselves now, Next I will share my new year’s resolutions to all.
  As we all know, keeping health is very important for everyone, I will keep the current exercise program insistently in order to achieving my goal, Last year I have kept running almost 10KM every day and it is extremely painful that really helped me lose weight and keep healthy. If I have the chance, I will share the experience about how to lose weight to audience, to give reference and confidence. I hope to keep current figure and build up muscles in this year. It is a huge challenge for me, so in the following days I will put fitness as a regular part of my life.
  In learning respect, I will read more books and running my blog. I seldom reading books during my university. But since I started working, I enjoy reading more and more. It is an excellent way to gain knowledge from all kinds of topics and a great exercise for my brain. But I have difficulties in memorizing what I read by only reading it. So I take notes when reading a book, discuss and exchange the ideas with my friends, then I will write the feelings about the books and post it to my blog. I have a personal interest to build up my own blog website. I bought an independent domain and a cloud server, to record meaningful stories in my life and technology articles. In the past year, updating my blog has become one of my habits in my daily life which brings many benefits to me. The most vital advantage is to communicate and help with other bloggers. Weibo and twitter can’t instead of it. Especially social network, English is extremely important for all. I really want to improve my speech skills and English spoken level. Personally, huge challenge can also be an inspiration.
  In a word, it looks like difficult to achieve these goals, maybe disappointed and even thought to give up when we do it. we should overcome our laziness and procrastination. the ancient said: preparedness ensures success, and unpreparedness spells failure. proved the importance of plan, so according to the resolutions to achieve goal is necessary, human always constantly struggle and improve ourselves in the whole life. Some people always complain that they are not lucky enough to meet good things or good people. However, I don’t think it’s because of luckiness but because of you. Therefore, try your best to become better, no matter in work, life or a relationship.

  • 值得肯定的点：稿子背得熟，不拘谨，得到会员们的一致肯定
  • 需要改善的点：英语发音以及语法措辞问题
